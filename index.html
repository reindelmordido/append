Features

PHONE SPECIFICATIONS

Phone specifications (or phone specs) refer to the technical details that define a smartphone's performance and features. Key specs include the processor (chipset and speed), RAM (memory for multitasking), and storage (capacity for apps and files). The display size, resolution, and refresh rate affect visual quality, while the camera specifications determine photo and video capabilities. Battery life is measured in mAh (milliamp-hours), and connectivity options include 4G/5G, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth. Other features like operating system, design, biometric security (fingerprint or face recognition), and audio quality also play a crucial role in defining a phone’s overall user experience.
